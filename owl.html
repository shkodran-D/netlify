Have you ever become thirsty while showering? Have you ever drunk a glass of water while relaxing in the tub? It’s invigorating! Because there is water everywhere, it would be quite convenient!

Curbie, a small owl, understood how handy it would be to receive a drink and a bath at the same time. Isn’t he adorable? The owner believed he was cute enough to upload this video to the internet for all to see! When everyone else realized how cute he was, it soon traveled around the internet.




Curbie flew over to his owner from where he was resting on the couch in the video. When the proprietor noticed Curbie needed a drink, she grabbed the spray bottle and sprayed it into his mouth. She’s familiar with the way he hoots when he’s thirsty. Curbie, on the other hand, didn’t want just that.

He stretched his wings out after he finished drinking so that he could be sprayed as well! He was desperate for a shower! He flung them open wide so he could wipe himself. He raises his wings as high as he can, indicating that he would like a few sprays under his wings before settling in for a nap.

He revolves in a circle to allow his owner to clean every blemish on him. When he’s finished with that, he spins around and sticks his butt out! He seemed to be finished after that. He flies away, satisfied with his owner’s cleaning effort, to get his sleep in.




He’s finished showering and drinking. Curbie appears to be adored by everyone, even his mother! On YouTube alone, he has over 200 thousand views.
